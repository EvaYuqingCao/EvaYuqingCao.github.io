Eva Yuqing Cao

Eva Yuqing Cao is a multimedia storyteller, comparatist and activist whose work crosses the border of the quantitative and the qualitative – converging art, literature, linguistics and pressing social issues with computer-science oriented technologies. Eva’s creativity combined with technological skills (Python, VR/AR, Html/Css/JavaScript, C++ for Unreal Engine, Adobe Creative Suite, Flixel, Corel VideoStudio) leads to her aspiration of designing connective media projects that could narrate human experiences and emotions ---technologies that encounter and solve human problems. With the experience of interviewing migrant workers in New Delhi and Beijing, the homeless in the Jungle (Ithaca’s tent city) during Covid and sexual assault survivors, Eva believes in the power of the human voice and storytelling. A “super-literature” is required, an avant-garde form of literature that’s close to connective media that uses technology to spread and amplify the human voice.
